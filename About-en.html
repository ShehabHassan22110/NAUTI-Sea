About Us

Nauti is the navigator steering the ship at sea, and with him, enthusiasts of maritime voyages embark on a unique journey for unparalleled nautical experiences, exclusively in the crystal-clear waters of the Red Sea. Here, the pristine waters of protected lagoons, tranquil sandy beaches, and nearby coral reefs offer havens that allow for unforgettable experiences, alongside an innovative entertainment concept with family or friends, for the first time in the Kingdom

Our services

Know about our services that captivate the senses

Individual

Our guests will enjoy a variety of shore-side and offshore activities ranging for the casual sunbathing lazy days, to the most adventurous sports fishing trips. Experiences include clear kayaks and stand-up paddles, underwater scooters, and many more unique water toys

Corporate

Corporate guests are invited to various dry or wet team-building activities that can be self-directed or through professional providers. Our facilities are also prepared to host corporate family days, including customized music and games programs

How we work

Integrated marine experiances...for all !

Beach Clubs - Shoreside and Floating

With premium designs, soothing atmospheres, and filled with activities, our beach club business integrates state-of-the-art equipment for a holistic experience for guests of all ages, from water toys to swimming pools and cabanas nestled amid crystal-clear waters .

Vessels - Pontoons, Boats, Jet skis, etc.

Our fleet encompasses vessels introduced to the local market for the first time. The double-decker pontoons offer stable rides with water slides, disabled persons’ cranes, and showers. Our crystal-clear electric boats for group explorations are most unique. We also provide sports fishing yachts, water sports jet boats, and jet skis

Retail - Branded water toys, beach accessories, ice boxes, etc.

Our carefully selected branded retail collection will enhance customer experience on the beach and in the water. Water scooters, SUPs, dry bags, and many other items are available for the first time. Our direct collaboration with factories ensures we deliver quality at competitive prices
