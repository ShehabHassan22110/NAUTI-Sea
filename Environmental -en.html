ENVIRONMENTAL PLEDGE

- At NAUTI we acknowledge the inherent beauty and ecological significance of the Red Sea, which serves as the backdrop of our beach club and marine operations. We recognize our responsibility to protect and maintain this unique marine environment, and we are committed to incorporating sustainable practices into our operations that minimize our environmental impact.

- We realize that environmental responsibility is a collective effort. We commit to doing our part, and we encourage our visitors, partners, and the wider community to join us in preserving and protecting the beautiful and precious environment of the Red Sea

Marine Life Conservation

We recognize the Red Sea as a vibrant ecosystem filled with diverse marine life. We pledge to contribute to the protection of all species through education programs, non-intrusive practices, and partnerships.

Waste Management

We are committed to minimizing waste at our beach club and marine operations. We encourage recycling and proper waste disposal among our guests and staff, ensuring the cleanliness of our facilities and the surrounding marine environment. Plastic usage will be strictly monitored and reduced to the maximum extent.

Eco-Friendly Operations

We aim to incorporate eco-friendly materials and practices in our operations, from the construction and maintenance of our facilities to the boats and toys we offer. This includes energy-efficient operations, the use of renewable resources, and the exploration of new technologies that can further reduce our environmental footprint.

Sustainable Tourism

We strive to offer services that promote sustainable tourism. We will provide eco- friendly options and guidelines for our guests on how to respect and care for our environment. We are committed to educating our visitors about the Red Sea's unique ecosystem and promoting responsible tourism practices

Community Engagement

We believe in active engagement with our local community to improve environmental awareness and participation in our sustainability efforts. We aim to offer educational programs, community cleanup initiatives, and other activities to foster a culture of environmental stewardship.

Continuous Improvement

We pledge to regularly review and update our environmental policies and practices. We will seek to continuously improve our performance and minimize our environmental impact, recognizing the evolving nature of environmental conservation
