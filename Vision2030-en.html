Vision 2030

Vision 2030 shows a dazzling and inspiring future picture, where modern technology meets humanity and sustainability. Innovations are embodied in every aspect of life,

Economic diversification

Nauti contributes to diversifying the economy of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia by investing in the tourism sector. By attracting visitors and generating revenue through boat rentals and beach club memberships, the operation creates jobs and stimulates economic growth

Tourism promotion

Nauti offers visitors a unique marine experience, enabling them to enjoy water sports, boat trips and relax on the beach. By providing such entertainment options, the operation contributes to the growth of the tourism sector in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia .

Job creation and skills development

As N auti expands its operations, it creates job opportunities in various sectors such as hospitality, boating services and customer service. By providing training and employment, the operation supports the development of a skilled workforce, in line with Vision 2030 objectives to enhance employment and capitalization . Human money .

Improved quality of life

Nauti's beach club facilities provide residents and visitors with a place to relax, socialize and enjoy leisure time. By offering a range of amenities, including beach access, swimming pools, restaurants and health services, the operation improves the quality of life for individuals and families in Saudi Arabia .

Sustainability and conservation

Nauti prioritizes environmental sustainability by implementing environmentally friendly practices. The operation focuses on responsible boating, marine conservation and waste management to reduce its environmental footprint. These efforts are consistent with Vision 2030’s focus on environmental preservation and sustainable development .

Nauti is the captain of your journey through the waves of the seas, guiding your ship with safety and confidence. With Nauti, the sailing experience becomes more enjoyable and beautiful.
